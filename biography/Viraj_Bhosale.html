Early Life

Viraj Bhosale was born on 2 January 2007 in Bidkin, Chhatrapati Sambhaji Nagar, Maharashtra, India. Since childhood, Viraj has been passionate about technology, creativity, and gaming. He showed early interest in coding, anime, and digital arts, which shaped his journey into graphic designing and computer science.

Education

Viraj completed his schooling in Maharashtra, where he developed strong skills in mathematics, programming, and creative arts. In 2025, he joined MGM University, Aurangabad to pursue a Bachelor of Technology in Computer Science, aiming to enhance his expertise in software development, artificial intelligence, and graphic design.

Gaming & Career
