Early Life

Jaydatt Khodave was born on 10 September 2006 in Parbhani, Maharashtra, India. From his school days, he was passionate about technology, problem-solving, and innovation. His curiosity in coding and digital tools eventually led him to create his own projects and later establish It Is Unique Official.

Education

Jaydatt completed his schooling in Maharashtra, where he developed a strong interest in mathematics, science, and computer programming. In 2025, he enrolled in MGM University, Aurangabad to pursue a Bachelor of Technology in Computer Science. His academic journey reflects his dedication to full-stack development, cybersecurity, and artificial intelligence research.

Career

As the Founder of It Is Unique Official, Jaydatt Khodave has built a digital solutions platform focusing on web development, SEO optimization, and open-source tools. He also leads Supreme X, an innovation-driven group focusing on scalable web platforms, ethical hacking, and security research. In addition, he manages projects like The Blaze Times (tech news portal) and iiuo.org (open-source tools platform).

Achievements

  • Founder of It Is Unique Official, a fast-growing tech solutions brand.
  • Leader of Supreme X, an innovation and research-focused community.
  • Created The Blaze Times, a digital news platform.
  • Built iiuo.org, an open-source utilities and tools project.
  • Contributed to GitHub projects under the username iamjaydatt.
  • Recognized for developing SEO-friendly, high-performance, and accessible websites.

Personal Life

Outside of professional life, Jaydatt enjoys writing blogs, mentoring students in programming and entrepreneurship, and experimenting with AI and cybersecurity research. He is also an advocate for open-source software and community-driven development.
