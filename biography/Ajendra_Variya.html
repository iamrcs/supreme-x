Early Life

Ajendra Variya, popularly known as Ajju Bhai, was born on 12 August 1998 in Ahmedabad, Gujrat, India. From a young age, he showed a keen interest in video games and digital platforms. He comes from a Gujarati Hindu family and completed schooling up to 12th grade before dropping out of a diploma program to focus on his gaming career.

Career & YouTube Journey

Ajendra started his gaming career with mobile games such as Clash of Clans but transitioned to Garena Free Fire in 2018, which became the turning point of his professional gaming journey. He launched his YouTube channel Total Gaming on 9 October 2018, uploading gaming highlights, live streams, and Free Fire gameplay. Over time, Total Gaming became one of India’s largest gaming channels, amassing over 40 million subscribers.

Apart from Total Gaming, he manages multiple other channels including Ajay Verse, TG Highlights, Total Gaming Shorts, and TG Tournaments. He also possesses technical skills in software development, including knowledge of PHP and JavaScript.

Achievements

  • Founder of the YouTube channel Total Gaming.
  • One of India’s largest professional gamers and content creators.
  • Manages multiple successful YouTube channels expanding gaming content.
  • Technical background in software development with knowledge of PHP and JavaScript.
  • Influential figure in the Indian gaming community, promoting competitive and mobile gaming.

Personal Life

Ajendra follows a vegetarian lifestyle, does not consume alcohol or smoke, and enjoys gaming professionally. He is passionate about creating gaming content, mentoring aspiring gamers, and innovating in the digital gaming space.
