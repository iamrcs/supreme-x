About

Jawaharlal Nehru Engineering College (JNEC) is a premier private institution located in Chhatrapati Sambhajinagar, Maharashtra. Established in 1983, it is a constituent college of MGM University. Accredited with an ‘A’ Grade by NAAC, JNEC offers a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and Ph.D. programs approved by AICTE and COA. The college is known for its academic excellence, modern infrastructure, and strong industry collaborations.

  • Hostels: Separate accommodation for boys and girls with AC & non-AC options.
  • Sports Complex: 400m synthetic athletics track, courts for cricket, football, basketball & more.
  • Innovation, Incubation & Research Centre (IIRC): In collaboration with Mahindra Tech & BMW.

  • Other: Labs, auditoriums, cafeteria, mess, medical clinic.

Admissions

For AY 2025–26, JNEC admissions are conducted directly through MGM University, not via CET CAP.

  1. Online registration via MGM University/JNEC website.
  2. Entrance exam or merit-based shortlisting.
  3. Counseling & document verification.
  4. Fee payment and admission confirmation.

Entrance Exams Accepted:

  • B.Tech: MGMU-CET, JEE Main, MHT-CET, PERA CET
  • B.Arch: NATA
  • M.Tech: GATE, MGMU-CET, PERA CET
  • MCA: MGMU CET, MAH-MCA-CET, PERA CET
