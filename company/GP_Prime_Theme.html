About

GP Prime Theme is a modern eCommerce platform specializing in WordPress themes, templates, plugins, and digital design resources. Founded by Abhishth Ramani on March 3, 2025, the platform focuses on delivering high‑quality products for businesses, developers, and creators. GP Prime Theme is affiliated with A(R) Official, making their offerings part of a network of trusted digital brands. With an emphasis on performance, flexibility, and customer experience, GP Prime Theme has positioned itself as a trusted source for premium web solutions.
