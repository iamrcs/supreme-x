About

It Is Unique Official is a tech organization based in Parbhani, Maharashtra, India. Founded by Jaydatt Khodave on 15 August 2023, the company delivers expert web development, blogging tools, and software solutions. With a strong focus on open-source innovation and educational resources, the organization builds developer tools, shares knowledge, and provides scalable digital solutions.

Services

  • Custom Website Development & Optimization
  • Progressive Web Apps (PWAs) – with or without offline support
  • SEO Enhancements & Performance Tuning
  • Business Automation & Digital Consulting
  • Content Management Systems
  • UPI QR & Payment Solutions
  • Link Shorteners & APK Distribution Platforms

Open-Source & Education

The organization actively contributes to the open-source community with projects such as the Telegram Storage Bot, educational blogging templates, and digital tools. It also shares guides on JavaScript, React, Node.js, reverse engineering, and more, helping students and developers grow their skills.
