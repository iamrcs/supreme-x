About

It Is Unique Official is a Parbhani-based technology organization founded on 15 August 2023 by Jaydatt Khodave. The company specializes in web development, automation, and open-source innovation. Through software solutions and educational resources, it empowers creators, students, and developers to build impactful digital experiences.

Services

  • Custom Website Development & Optimization
  • Progressive Web Apps (PWAs)
  • SEO Enhancements & Performance Tuning
  • Automation Tools & Digital Consulting
  • Content Management Systems (CMS)
  • UPI Payment & QR Solutions
  • Link Shorteners & APK Hosting Platforms

Open Source & Education

It Is Unique Official contributes to open-source through educational templates, developer bots, and free resources such as the Telegram Storage Bot. It also creates tutorials on JavaScript, React, Node.js, and modern web technologies to help learners advance in programming and digital innovation.
