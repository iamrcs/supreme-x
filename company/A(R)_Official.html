About

A(R) Official is a digital company based in Tilda Neora, Chhattisgarh, India. Founded by Abhishth Ramani on 23 January 2024, the company provides WordPress templates, hosting services, plugins, and theme design solutions. With a strong focus on performance, customization, and customer support, A(R) Official helps individuals, businesses, and developers build scalable and creative digital products.

Services

  • Premium & Custom WordPress Theme Design
  • Web Hosting & Domain Services
  • Plugin Development & Distribution
  • eProduct Store for Templates & Plugins
  • Theme Customization & Technical Support
  • Performance Optimization & SEO

Community & eCommerce

A(R) Official is closely connected with GPPrimeTheme.com, an eCommerce store for selling templates, plugins, and digital products. Through this platform, the organization provides developers and businesses with ready-to-use, high-quality digital tools to accelerate their web projects.
