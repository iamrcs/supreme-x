About

Zoho Corporation is a privately held, global cloud-based software company founded in 1996 (originally as AdventNet, Inc.) by Sridhar Vembu and Tony Thomas. Headquartered in Chennai, India, Zoho operates worldwide with major offices in the U.S. and other regions. The company offers an integrated suite of SaaS products including Zoho CRM, Zoho One, Zoho Books, Zoho Mail, Zoho Projects, Zoho Creator, and more, focusing on productivity, collaboration, finance, HR, IT management, and business automation. Zoho is privately held, largely bootstrapped, and has grown to ~15,000 employees with estimated revenue of ~US$1.4B (2024).
