About

MGM University is a self-financed private state university located in Chhatrapati Sambhajinagar (formerly Aurangabad), Maharashtra. Established by the Mahatma Gandhi Mission Trust — a reputed educational trust with over four decades of excellence — the university was founded on January 1, 1982 and gained state university status through the MGM University Act, 2019. It holds UGC 2(f) status and is accredited with ‘A’ grade by NAAC.

Faculties & Programs

  • Engineering & Technology – B.Tech (CSE, AI & ML, Data Science, Robotics)
  • Management & Commerce – MBA, BBA (Hons.), B.Com (Hons.)
  • Basic & Applied Sciences – B.Sc & M.Sc (Forensic Science, Computer Science, Food Technology)
  • Law – LLB, Integrated BBA LLB
  • Design & Performing Arts – B.Des, BFA with filmmaking and photography facilities
  • Medicine & Dentistry, Nursing & Physiotherapy, Bioscience & Technology, and more.

Admission Process

  1. Online application and registration
  2. Entrance exam (JEE Main, MHT CET, MGMU-CET, NATA, NEET, CAT, etc.) or merit-based shortlisting
  3. Counseling & document verification
  4. Fee payment and admission confirmation
