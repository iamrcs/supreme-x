Jaydatt Khodave Launches Innovative Tech Initiative Under It Is Unique Official

Published on October 2, 2025 | By Team Supreme X

Chhatrapati Sambhajinagar, 2 October 2025: Jaydatt Khodave, the visionary founder of It Is Unique Official, has launched an innovative technology initiative designed to empower startups, entrepreneurs, and young innovators across India. The initiative aims to bridge gaps in technology, mentorship, and funding for promising ideas and projects.

Key Objectives of the Initiative:

  • Startup Mentorship: Provide guidance and resources for emerging tech entrepreneurs.
  • Innovation Workshops: Conduct sessions to enhance technical and creative skills.
  • Funding Support: Offer seed funding for promising tech projects.
  • Networking Opportunities: Connect innovators with industry leaders and investors.
  • Recognition & Awards: Showcase successful projects through media coverage and awards.
